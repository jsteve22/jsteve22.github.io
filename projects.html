Homomorphic Encryption Model

This is part of my research during the Summer of 2022. This is a simple test checking the validity of multiplying two cyphertexts. This also shows the computation costs for ciphertext mutplication and which functions are affected most.

Chess Engine

This chess engine was written in Golang. I used resources from chessprogramming.com to validate correct move generation. This project is on hold currently, but in the future I hope to rewrite some of the logic to increase the speed of move generation.

VPP Text Editor

This is the final project my team developed for CSE20312, Data Structures. This project was written in C++. I worked in a team with three other CS students. VPP is a simplified text editor that is compatible with the student machines at ND. We envisioned it as a way to ease CS students into more advanced editors such as Vim or Emacs.
